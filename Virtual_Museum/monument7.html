The Mall

The Mall gardens fountain is located in Floriana. The fountain is situated in the center of the garden and is a popular spot for tourists and locals.

The 18th-century fountain has three tiers of basins with water falling down from one to the next. A statue of a boy holding a dolphin, a sign of abundance and success, is perched above the fountain.The baroque style, which was prominent in Europe during the 17th and 18th centuries, is reflected in the fountain's design. It is surrounded by a well-kept garden, which gives guests a calm and unwinding ambiance.

The Mall gardens fountain is one of the many historical landmarks in Valletta, and is eleborate design and beautiful surroundings make it a popular spot for photography and sightseeing.
